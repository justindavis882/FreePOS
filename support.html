Quick Setup Guide

Get your terminal up and running in under 5 minutes. No hardware configuration required.

1

Create Your Account

Sign up using your email or Google account from the main terminal page. You will instantly be placed on the 100% Free Core Tier.

2

Configure Settings

Click the ⚙️ icon in the top right. Set your Store Name, customized Receipt Header, and your local Sales Tax Rate.

3

Add Inventory

Click the "Products" button. You can add up to 25 items for free. Assign colors to make them easy to find on the grid.

4

Ring Up a Sale

Tap items to add them to your ticket. Click "Charge" to calculate change or select a card payment, and automatically generate a digital/printable receipt.
