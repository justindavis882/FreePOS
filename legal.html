2. Privacy Policy

We believe in keeping your data secure and staying out of your way.

What We Collect

We collect essential account details (Name, Email, Store Name) to manage your terminal instance. When utilizing digital payment features, transaction data is routed securely through Stripe. FreePOS does not store full credit card numbers on our servers.

Offline Caching

To provide offline capabilities, FreePOS utilizes your device's local browser storage. Clearing your browser data before an internet connection is re-established will result in permanent loss of offline transaction records. We do not track you across third-party websites.

3. Hardware Refund & Return Policy

Note: This policy applies only to physical hardware and supplies ordered directly through the FreePOS fulfillment center.

  • 30-Day Window: Unopened and unused hardware (terminals, receipt paper, cash drawers) can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund minus shipping costs.
  • Defective Units: If your hardware arrives dead-on-arrival (DOA) or malfunctions due to a manufacturing defect within 90 days, we will replace it at no cost.
